
- User has existing content
- Needs optimization, not creation
- Single skill execution: geo-optimize

### Step 2: Execute

**Executing geo-optimize...**

**Analysis:**
- Current structure: Good
- Missing elements: FAQ section, clear definitions, citations
- Optimization opportunities: 5

**Optimizations Applied:**

1. **Added FAQ Section**
   - 5 common questions answered
   - Increases chance of being cited

2. **Enhanced Definitions**
   - Clear, quotable definitions for key terms
   - Makes content more citable

3. **Improved Structure**
   - Added clear H2/H3 hierarchy
   - Better for AI parsing

4. **Added Citations**
   - Linked to authoritative sources
   - Increases credibility

5. **Optimized Opening**
   - Clear, concise summary
   - Better for AI extraction

**Expected Impact:**
- +30% chance of being mentioned
- +50% chance of being cited
- Better performance in informational queries

**Next Steps:**
1. Publish optimized version
2. Monitor for 2 weeks
3. Measure improvement in mentions

## Example 4: Competitive Response

**User Input:**
```
My competitors have been performing well in AI search recently. How should I respond?
```

**CMO Execution:**

### Step 1: Analyze Competitor

**Executing competitor-analyze...**

Results:
- Competitor Answer Share: 40% (up from 25% last month)
- Strong in: Comparison queries, "best X" queries
- Weak in: Technical queries, implementation guides

### Step 2: Find Your Opportunities

**Executing geo-audit...**

Your current state:
- Answer Share: 15%
- Strong in: Technical content
- Weak in: Comparison content, recommendations

### Step 3: Strategic Response

**Competitive Response Strategy:**

**Immediate Actions (Week 1-2):**
1. **[HIGH]** Create "Your Brand vs Competitor" comparison
   - Leverage their visibility to get mentioned
   - Honest, fair comparison
   - Target: Published in 1 week

2. **[HIGH]** Optimize existing technical content
   - You're already strong here
   - Make it even better
   - Target: 3 articles optimized

**Short-term (Week 3-6):**
3. **[MEDIUM]** Create "Best X" recommendation content
   - Include your brand prominently
   - Provide genuine value
   - Target: 2 articles

4. **[MEDIUM]** Build FAQ content
   - Answer common questions
   - Highly citable format
   - Target: 20 Q&As

**Long-term (Month 2-3):**
5. **[LOW]** Differentiate on technical depth
   - Competitor is weak here
   - Your strength
   - Create comprehensive guides

**Expected Outcome:**
- Week 2: +3-5% Answer Share (comparison content)
- Week 6: +5-8% Answer Share (recommendation content)
- Month 3: +10-15% Answer Share (technical differentiation)
- Target: 30% Answer Share (competitive with leader)

**Key Insight:**
Don't just copy competitor — leverage your technical strength while addressing comparison gap.
